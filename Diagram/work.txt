n Simple Terms, How Does the System Work?

Companies register on the website and post job openings
Job seekers create accounts and add their personal information and skills
Job seekers search for suitable jobs and apply for them
The system tracks job applications and their statuses
Jobs and job seekers can be filtered by skills and geographic regions

This diagram is like a map showing how information is organized and stored in the job system's database, and how this information is connected to facilitate the process of searching for and applying to jobs.
